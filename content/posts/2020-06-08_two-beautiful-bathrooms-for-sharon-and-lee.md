# Two Beautiful Bathrooms for Sharon and Lee

These two beautiful bathrooms were started before lockdown but due to restrictions put in place we couldn’t get all of the equipment delivered. It’s been lovely to work for Sharon and Lee in this ground floor apartment. We would of loved to get the trays lower but had to be built up due the drainage in the apartment.It’s take a lot longer than expected but finally done. All bathrooms fully decorated straight away so no hassle for the customer complete start to finish product
