# Stepping up to Deliver a Quality bathroom for Rachel

Stepping up to deliver a quality bathroom for Rachel Aldersley. After Rachel and family were let down by another company down weeks before their scheduled start date. She asked on a local page for recommendations, five weeks later rachel went away on Holiday and left me and the team to it. This is the finished product. The lads worked late at night to ensure our customers already booked in during the days were not inconvenienced or their builds impacted. What a proud achievement to deliver this stunning bathroom under pressure. Thank you for putting your trust in me after only three visits and as promised we would deliver
