# All the Bathroom mod cons for Hayley and Mike

A stunning bathroom refurbishment… for the wonderful Hayley and Mike! They arrived home from a lovely holiday in Mexico to this turnaround, complete with a new cylinder cupboard area built to house their new pressurised cylinder, and home boiler. A free standing bath with all the trimmings. Installation of ceiling speakers that have audio to a 19’ waterproof TV… just to add to all the mod cons, we installed a Bluetooth audio mirror!
