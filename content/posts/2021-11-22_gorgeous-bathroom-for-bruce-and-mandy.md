# Gorgeous Bathroom for Bruce and Mandy

What a gorgeous bathroom finished today ( …for the breakfast specialists ) Bruce and his lovely wife, Mandy. Bruce contacted me a year ago, he had a project but Mandy wanted it her way (and only her way!). Guess what? We delivered just as imagined once again!! The Body Shop guru herself, Mandy, knew exactly what she wanted. This bathroom has been fitted out in 1200x600 marble effect porcelain, with a free standing 1500x800 bath. This has been such an gorgeous bathroom project to work on.
