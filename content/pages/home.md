## Section 2: Three room-type cards

**Bathroom**

Your whole family uses your bathroom on a daily basis.
You might not invite overnight guests into your bedroom but you certainly wouldn't deprive them access to your bathroom.

This bathroom should be the standard to which your cloakrooms and ensuites aspire to.

Huge bath or walk in wet room, this is your preference and anything is possible. 
Choose your chinaware from our or any range and then we can provide options to compliment.

**Cloakroom**

Family, friends and general visitors will probably see this room. 

When entertaining or relaxing you shouldn't need to take the stairs to find facilities of a high standard. 
 

Half height tiles and a decorated finish to match the rest of your house provides a perfect balance whilst maintaining a spacious feel. 

When you feel like a change a simple lick of paint can refresh without breaking the bank.

**en suite**

The en-suite is a private space, a tranquil area designed specifically for your relaxation.

It's an opportunity to create amazing, unique area. Previous projects have included everything from the standard his & hers sinks to jacuzzi baths, steam rooms and everything in-between.

This is your space and we take great pride in making it perfect.
