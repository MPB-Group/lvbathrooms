Three elegant bathrooms finished today, pushed over the line in one month without any sacrifice to specification or quality! …with a fourth prepped and ready to go as soon as the flooring has been fitted throughout the rest of the house. The Wainwright family contacted me January 2020 for a 4 bathroom quote, unfortunately due to Covid the job had to be delayed. We have completely gutted every single bathroom, without leaving any old floorboards or walls in… We replaced everything so we know it is all brand new, start to finish! Every bathroom has a new waste from every appliance to the new stack outside. Every bathroom has - Porcelain tiles on the wall and floorFully sound proofed walls and floorsUnderfloor heatingNew electrics The whole job was supplied and designed by the team @Smithfields of Stockton Heath. We have gone above and beyond to pull these bathrooms out of the bag to finish on schedule.
